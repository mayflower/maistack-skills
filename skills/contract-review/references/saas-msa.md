# SaaS / MSA Review Notes

Use this reference for SaaS subscriptions, MSAs, service agreements, SOW-backed platform deals, reseller paper, and related DPAs or security schedules.

## Focus Areas

- Liability cap level and carve-outs
- Indemnities: IP, data/security, bodily injury, confidentiality, fees
- Termination rights, cure periods, and suspension language
- Renewal notice windows and price-increase caps
- SLA, service credits, support commitments, and outage remedies
- Data ownership, export, deletion, and transition assistance
- Security commitments, subprocessors, audit rights, breach notice
- Assignment and change-of-control restrictions

## Common Red Flags

- Liability cap tied to 3 months of fees on annual or multi-year spend
- No uptime commitment or no remedy for SLA failures
- Unilateral vendor suspension or termination rights
- Uncapped renewal price increases
- Weak data export rights or no post-termination access window
- Broad vendor use rights over customer data

## DPA / Privacy Addendum Notes

- If the review centers on controller/processor allocation, SCCs, TOMs, subprocessors, retention, or international transfers, combine this skill with `gdpr-dsgvo-expert`.
- Call out when the paper mixes commercial terms and privacy obligations in a way that creates conflicting precedence.

## Typical Redline Directions

- Raise liability cap toward 12 months of fees or a floor amount
- Add mutual or better-balanced termination and cure rights
- Add SLA commitments, credits, and material-breach thresholds
- Add data export, deletion, and transition-assistance language
- Cap renewal price increases and lengthen renewal notice windows
