# M&A Review Notes

Use this reference for asset purchases, stock purchases, merger agreements, diligence-driven customer-contract reviews, earnout documents, and disclosure schedules.

## Focus Areas

- Cash at close vs earnout / holdback / escrow mix
- Representation survival periods and which reps are fundamental
- Basket structure: deductible vs tipping / first-dollar
- Indemnification cap and fraud carve-outs
- Earnout mechanics, accounting standard, audit rights, anti-manipulation language
- Sandbagging / anti-sandbagging
- Restrictive covenants and their duration / geography
- Working capital adjustment process
- Change-of-control and assignment effects in target contracts

## Seller-Side Red Flags

- Buyer calculates earnout in its sole discretion
- 24-36 month survival for all reps
- Broad pro-sandbagging clause
- Five-year non-compete with nationwide scope
- First-dollar basket instead of true deductible

## Customer-Contract Diligence Lens

When reviewing signed customer contracts in a transaction:

- note change-of-control consent requirements,
- flag MFN, exclusivity, unusual SLA credits, or one-off indemnity terms,
- identify renewals or termination windows that affect valuation,
- call out data-processing annexes that may require separate privacy review.
